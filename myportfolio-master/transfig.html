Self Portait


As both a consumer and designer, I noticed makeup packaging falls short in functionality and aesthetics. Traveling with makeup means buying duplicate products, which is costly and inconvenient. The inconsistent design and lack of creativity across brands also make it challenging to organize and uninspiring from a design perspective. I aimed to address these issues by creating a sculptural, modular makeup packaging solution that merges functionality and beauty.

My Hair Continued


I drew inspiration from vintage perfumery, where ornate bottles served both decorative and practical functions. Iconic tabletop lighters from brands like Edie Parker, once reserved for elite settings, influenced my approach to recontextualize everyday items with artistic flair. Other inspirations included the sculptural lip balm packaging by Salwashoo and Cadence’s magnetic travel containers, both of which add aesthetic and functional elements to standard designs.
