For this project, I was tasked with creating cover art for a DJ's new music single. The DJ wanted a design that was eye-catching, modern, and conveyed a sense of excitement and energy.

I began by researching current design trends in the music industry, analyzing the DJ's brand identity, and providing reference material. From there, I created several different design concepts and presented them to the DJ for feedback. I was also inspired by sound wave visualization and gestalt design theory, principles/laws of human perception that describe how humans group similar elements.

The final design features bold, maroon-colored typography and designs that pop against a dark, abstract background. The designs include various shapes and patterns, giving the design a dynamic and energetic feel. I also incorporated a small wordmark logo for DJ to reinforce the branding.

Overall, I believe the design successfully captures the DJ's brand identity and conveys a sense of excitement and energy, making it a perfect fit for the music single. I'm proud of the work I did on this project and believe it would make a great addition to my portfolio.
